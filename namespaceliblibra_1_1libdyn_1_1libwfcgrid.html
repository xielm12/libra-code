Initialize a Gaussian wavepacket on a 2D grid

Parameters
[out]wfcIs a list of complex matrices (vectors), each containing numerical wavefunction for different electronic state
[in]Xis the complex matrix(vector) containing the grid points along X direction
[in]x_Position of the center of the Gaussian wavepacket in X direction
[in]px_Momentum of the Gaussian wavepacket in X direction
[in]dxSpread (distribution width) of the spatial component of the Gaussian wavepacket along the axis X
[in]Yis the complex matrix(vector) containing the grid points along Y direction
[in]y_Position of the center of the Gaussian wavepacket in Y direction
[in]py_Momentum of the Gaussian wavepacket in Y direction
[in]dySpread (distribution width) of the spatial component of the Gaussian wavepacket along the axis Y
[in]nstatesThe number of electronic states for which to initialize the wavefunction
[in]occ_stateIndex of the occupied electronic state on which the wavepacket is initialized

G(x,y) = G(x)*G(y) G(x) = [ (1/(2.0*pi*dx^2))^(1/4) ] * exp(-((x-x_)/(2*dx))^2 + i*((x-x_)/dx)*px_) G(y) = [ (1/(2.0*pi*dy^2))^(1/4) ] * exp(-((y-y_)/(2*dy))^2 + i*((y-y_)/dy)*py_)

Initialize 1D grid

The number of points that is a power of 2 and encloses the selected interval with the given spacing between the points is computed, and then these points will be generated as a Nx x 1 complex-valued matrix (elements are actually real-valued)

Parameters
[in]xminThe minimal (leftmost) boundary of the grid
[in]xmaxThe maximal (rightmost) boundary of the grid
[in]dxThe spacing between the grid points.
